Download The ALPBench Benchmark Suite (Version 1.0)

Download all ALPBench applications, including a report characterizing their parallelism and performance.

Download individual applications and report:

  • SpeechRec
    • Derived from CMU Sphinx 3.3
    • Key modifications: Multithreading, SSE2

  • FaceRec
    • Derived from CSU Face Recognizer
    • Key modifications: Multithreading, SSE2

  • RayTrace
    • Unchanged Tachyon Raytracer (includes multithreading, SSE2 is not applicable)

  • MPGenc
    • Derived from MSSG MPEG-2 encoder
    • Key modifications: Multithreading, SSE2, three-step motion search, use of use of improved IDCT and DCT.

  • MPGdec
    • Derived from MSSG MPEG-2 decoder
    • Key modifications: Multithreading, SSE2, use of improved IDCT.

  • Paper characterizing the parallelism and performance of the above applications
