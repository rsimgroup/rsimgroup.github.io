Publications for NSF Award Number CCR-0205638
“ITR: Collaborative Hardware-Software Adaptation for Multimedia Applications”
PI: Sarita V. Adve
CoPIs: Robin Kravets, Douglas L. Jones, Klara Nahrstedt

The following publications are referenced in the annual report for 2002-03. Hard copies have been mailed to NSF.

  • Energy-Efficient Soft Real-Time CPU Scheduling for Mobile Multimedia Systems. Wanghong Yuan and Klara Nahrstedt. To appear in the Proceedings of the Symposium on Operating Systems Principles (SOSP'03), Bolton Landing, NY, October 2003.

  • Adaptive Video Coding to Reduce Energy on Adaptive General-Purpose Processors. Daniel G. Sachs, Sarita V. Adve, Douglas L. Jones. To appear in the Proceedings of the International Conference on Image Processing 2003 (ICIP '03), Barcelona, Spain, September 2003.

  • Buffering Approach for Energy Saving in Video Sensors. Wanghong Yuan and Klara Nahrstedt. To appear in the Proceedings of IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo (ICME2003), Baltimore, MD, July, 2003.

  • GRACE: A Hierarchical Adaptation Framework for Saving Energy. Daniel Grobe Sachs, Wanghong Yuan, Christopher J. Hughes, Albert Harris, Sarita V. Adve, Douglas L. Jones, Robin H. Kravets, and Klara Nahrstedt. Submitted for publication to IEEE Computer, 2003.

  • Providing Predictable Performance vs. Energy/Temperature Tradeoffs for Multimedia Applications. Christopher J. Hughes, Ruchira Sasanka, Jayanth Srinivasan, and Sarita V. Adve. Submitted for publication to IEEE Computer, 2003.

  • Spreading Slack for Optimal Energy-Performance Tradeoffs for Multimedia Applications. Christopher J. Hughes and Sarita V. Adve. Submitted for publication to Micro'03. Also available as UIUCDCS-R-2003-2358, June 2003.

  • Comparing the Energy Efficiency of CMP and SMT Architectures for Multimedia Workloads. Ruchira Sasanka, Sarita V. Adve, Yen-Kuang Chen, and Eric Debes. Submitted for publication to Micro'03. Also available as UIUCDCS-R-2003-2325, March 2003.

  • ReCalendar: Calendaring and Scheduling Applications with CPU and Energy Resource Guarantees for Mobile Devices. Wanghong Yuan and Klara Nahrstedt. Proceedings of the IEEE International Conference on Pervasive Computing and Communications (PerCom 2003), Dallas-Fort Worth, Texas, March 2003.

  • Design and Evaluation of A Cross-Layer Adaptation Framework for Mobile Multimedia Systems. Wanghong Yuan, Klara Nahrstedt, Sarita V. Adve, Douglas L. Jones, and Robin H. Kravets. Proceedings of the SPIE Conference on Multimedia Computing and Networking, January 2003.

  • Soft Real-Time Scheduling on a Simultaneous Multithreaded Processor. Rohit Jain, Christopher J. Hughes, and Sarita V. Adve. Proceedings of the 23rd IEEE International Real-Time Systems Symposium (RTSS-2002), December 2002.
